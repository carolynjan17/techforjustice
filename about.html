This is a static html prototype of a website to help people fill out government forms. The use case is people in DC who are eligible to have their records expunged. The site's intended audience are persons with a low level of literacy. For this use case the document is broken into several sections. There is a text explanation as well as a video to guide the person in creating the document. This site has a page that shows a sample of a completed motion for expungement.
