TECH FOR JUSTICE









The above video gives instructions on filling in the case(s) information. You can add more than one case. When you finished inputting the data click on the "Add Case buttton.

1. Docket ID: Enter the docket number for the case you are trying to expunge (cover/seal).

2. Charged With: Write what the police said you did. Do not try to argue that what they said was wrong. Simply write what they said you did.

3. Police Description: Again, just write what the police said you did.

4. Case Conclusion: Write a sentence how the case ended.

5. Case Pending: You should select "No". If the case is still pending and it is not a minor case then stop! You cannot submit this document until you have no cases pending.

6. End Date: Enter the date case was closed

7. Click on the "Add Case" button to add more cases.
