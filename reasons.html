For this section say why it would be best to have your record expunged.
A questionaire follows to help you get started. Answer those questions that apply to you. Please use complete sentences.

JOB
Do you have a job? Yes/No

If yes:
Where are you working?
What is your job title?
What are your duties/responsibilities?
How long have you had this job?
Can you fully support yourself with this job?
Does this job require a security clearance (example: public trust)?
Will you lose this job if you can't get cleared?

If no:
Have you been turned down for a job because of your record?
Have you been denied a security clearance?
Have you lost a job because you can't get cleared?
Have you lost a job because of your record?


EDUCATION
Are you in school? Yes/No
Are you in an educational program? Yes/No

If yes to either question:
Where are you in school?
What educational program are you in?
How long have you been the program?

If no to either question:
Did you apply and were denied entry into a school/education program?
Was your record the reason?
What did they say specifically?



MILITARY
Have you applied to serve in a branch of the military? Yes/No
Were you accepted? Yes/No

If no:
Were you turned down because of your record?
What reason did they give?


HOUSING
Do you live in your own place? Yes/No

If no:
Have you applied for an apt., housing?
Were you turned down?
Were you turned down because of your record?
